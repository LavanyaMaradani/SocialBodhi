Directions

Do you have a decision you need to make? Can't decide which movie to see or whether to break-up with someone? Get your friends to help!

  • Step 1: Tell me what choice you have to make ("Should you break-up or not break-up?")
  • Step 2: Tell me whom you want to ask (Enter their emails or pick their Facebook IDs.)
  • Step 3: Tell me how many rounds to go (The more important the decision, the more cycles of decision-and-elimination you should pick!)

That's it! Give the www.SocialBodhi.com decision engine a go!

FAQ

  • Is this just another poll or survey?
    • Not at all! A poll forces participants to choose a single option, even if they are equally split between two or more choices. Surveys act exactly like polls in only one dimension: they provide information once only but leave the actual decision-making process unknown. SocialBodhi is a decision engine that not only allows for a greater granularity of options but fully utilizes it to help come to the optimal group consensus instead of a simple majority vote.
  • What the heck's a bodhi?
    • Bodhi means Enlighentment or Awakening; SocialBodhi is a Social Awakening/Enlightenment for any question or decision in your life! Just like the 35 Buddhas or the 5 Wisdom Buddhas, use your friends' wisdom to free yourself from the cycle of decision and indecision by using your friends and family!
